Why Choose QTube?

YouTube Downloads

Download videos from YouTube in multiple quality options. Save your favorite content for offline viewing.

MP3 Extraction

Extract audio from videos and save as MP3. Perfect for creating your music library or podcast collection.

Cross-Platform

Works seamlessly on macOS, Windows, and Linux. Built with Electron and Quasar for a native experience.

Auto Updates

Stay up to date with automatic updates. Get the latest features and improvements without manual downloads.

Contribute to QTube

QTube is open source and welcomes contributions. Fork the repository and help make it better!
